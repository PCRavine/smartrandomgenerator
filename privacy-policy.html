Privacy Policy

Smart Random Generator Pro

Last Updated: December 11, 2024

Overview

Smart Random Generator Pro ("the App") is a tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) utility app developed by Blindrise Studio that helps users create and roll random tables, combos, and dice.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT use advertising or ad networks
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT require account creation or login
No Analytics No Ads No Tracking 100% Offline 100% Private

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Random tables Your custom table content Device only
Combos Your combo templates Device only
Roll history Record of past rolls Device only
App preferences Theme, settings Device only

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted over the internet, is not accessible to us or any third party, and can be deleted at any time.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We have no access to your data
  • No internet connection is required to use the App
  • Uninstalling the App removes all associated data

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage or sync services

Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Storage/Files Import/export tables and history Optional
Camera Scan text from images (OCR) Optional

These permissions are only used for the specific features described and do not transmit any data externally.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with the App:

  • Clear App Data: Go to Settings > Apps > Smart Random Generator Pro > Storage > Clear Data
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data
