Privacy Policy

Smart Dice Roller by Blindrise Studio

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

Summary: Smart Dice Roller is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App works entirely offline and all your data stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect No Tracking

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Any data from your device's sensors, camera, or microphone

Data Storage

All data created within the App, including:

  • Character profiles
  • Saved macros and dice rolls
  • App preferences and settings

is stored locally on your device only using standard Android storage mechanisms. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Smart Dice Roller does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Google Play In-App Purchases

If you choose to make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Smart Dice Roller does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device lock screen protection
  • Being cautious when installing apps from unknown sources

Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App from your device, or
  • Clearing the App's data through your device's Settings > Apps > Smart Dice Roller > Clear Data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your data. However, since Smart Dice Roller does not collect any personal data, these rights are automatically fulfilled:

  • Right to Access: There is no personal data to access
  • Right to Deletion: No personal data is stored on our servers
  • Right to Portability: All your data is already on your device
